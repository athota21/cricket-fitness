✅ Yes — this plan IS a weight loss program
Every element — calorie deficit, morning workouts, protein-heavy chicken days, strength training, and cardio — works together for fat loss. Here's exactly how:
◆ The 6 Pillars of Fat Loss
🔥
Calorie Deficit
Burning more than you eat. Target: 1,900 kcal/day vs ~2,500 TDEE = 600 kcal gap daily.
🏋️
Strength Training
Builds muscle that burns calories at rest. More muscle = higher metabolism = more fat burned 24/7.
🌅
Morning Cardio
Working out after just a banana keeps insulin low and targets fat stores more effectively.
🍗
High Protein Diet
Protein (especially chicken) keeps you full, prevents muscle loss, and has the highest thermic effect.
😴
Sleep 7–8 Hours
Poor sleep raises cortisol and ghrelin. Sleep is when fat burns and muscle rebuilds.
💧
Hydration
Drinking 3–4L/day boosts metabolism and prevents false hunger signals.
